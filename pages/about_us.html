About us

Bringing real food back to your table

Roots Company Ltd is an international company based in Taiwan that was born from the strategic vision that its founders share with a group of leading Argentinian food companies in the South American market. Working together with farmers, offering logistics, financial solutions, quality support, and technical development, are the key to our success and superior quality products. We take care of our customers with an open dialogue to understand their needs and satisfy their requirements. The members of Roots Company Ltd work together with the purpose of combining energies to bring our high-quality food products to Asia. Our main goal is to ensure that families can enjoy delicious and healthy meals, from a world class famous agricultural land as the Argentinean one.

Mission

Provide our customers with natural food from South America with added value from our farms and producers that can satisfy our client needs and standards, through personalized attention, efficient service, permanent training and innovative solutions for our internal processes. We provide the most efficient service from the farming land and producers warehouse until goods are delivered at final destination.

Vision

To make Roots Company Ltd., a reference supplier of Latin American food products in the international market following the international standards as a way of ensuring high quality in all our products and services provided.

Values

Customer Commitment

We develop relationships that make a positive difference in our customers’ lives

Quality

We provide outstanding healthy products and a personalized service that, together, deliver premium value to our customers.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all of our actions

Teamwork

We work together with our partners, across boundaries, to meet the needs of our customers and to achieve a common benefit
